Dear Parent,

To further simplify the payment of your ward’s fees, we have integrated a new payment gateway directly to the school ERP.

For this, you need to login with your User ID and Password in the space provided on our Home Page, and thereafter, in ERP follow these steps.

Fee -> Online Fee Payment -> Tick on I INSTALMENT -> PROCEED

You will be taken to the payment gateway, where you can use one of the suitable modes to pay the due amount online.

About GGVM Patna

GGVM Educational Society laid the foundation of veritable learning in Patna by establishing Guru Gyan Vidya Mandir School in the year 2019 in a collaborative effort with the RGC Schools Society, which is one of the largest chains of CBSE schools in India. Ggvm Patna is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi and offers schooling from Nursery to Class X. The last twenty three years have been a journey where perseverance and progress have been the cornerstones. Today it can proudly claim to be one of the most sought after schools in Eastern India.
